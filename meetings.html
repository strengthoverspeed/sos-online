Meetings

Free, peer-facilitated support groups every week — in person in Seattle and online. No registration required. Just show up.

First Time?

What to expect at your first meeting

Click here to review our Group Agreements

👋

Arrive as you are

You don’t need to have quit, have a sobriety date, or commit to anything. Just come and listen. You can share as much or as little as you want.

💬

Open conversation

Unlike 12-step meetings, we don’t have cross-talk rules. You can respond to others, ask questions, and engage in real dialogue.

🔒

Confidential

What’s shared in the group stays in the group. SOS is a judgment-free space. Names and stories are kept private.

In-Person Location

Peer Seattle

1520 Bellevue Ave, Suite 100
Seattle, WA

Peer Seattle is located on Capitol Hill. The building is accessible by bus and is near Seattle’s LGBTQ+ community hub.

Meetings are held in a private, welcoming space. No one will see you walking in or out except other SOS members.

Questions about getting there? Email sosonlinemeetings@gmail.com and someone will help you plan your first visit.

Online Meetings

Join from anywhere

Our online meetings are open to anyone, anywhere. If you’re outside of Seattle, struggling with transportation, or simply prefer the privacy of joining from home, online meetings are a great option.

Online meetings use the same open-discussion format as in-person groups. A webcam is helpful but not required.

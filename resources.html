Community Resources

There are many ways to get support beyond SOS. Find crisis lines, counseling, support groups, HIV testing, and more.

🚨

Need help right now?

Crisis Line (24 hours): 206-461-3222 • Alcohol/Drug Help Line: 206-722-3700  or  1-800-562-1240

Want to be listed here? If you are a community organization that supports gay and queer men in recovery, please contact us at sosonlinemeetings@gmail.com.

Support Groups

  • Gay & Sober Online community and resources for LGBTQ+ people in recovery. gayandsober.org
  • SMART Recovery (Non-12 Step) Science-based addiction recovery program as an alternative to 12-step. smartrecovery.org
  • Crystal Meth Anonymous www.crystalmeth.org
  • Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) — Seattle www.seattleaa.org
  • Narcotics Anonymous (NA) — Seattle www.seattlena.org

Counseling & Treatment

  • Project Neon Free one-on-one counseling, support groups, and health info for crystal users. gaycity.org/project-neon
  • Gay City: Resource Navigator Comprehensive directory of LGBTQ+ affirming services in the Seattle area. gaycity.org
  • Community Based Research Centre (CBRC) Research and publications on health issues affecting gay and bisexual men. cbrc.net/reports_and_publications
  • Entre Hermanos 206-322-7700 Support and resources for the Latino LGBTQ+ community. www.entrehermanos.org
  • POCAAN 206-322-7061 Community-based agency serving people of color. www.pocaan.org

Crisis & Help Lines

  • Crisis Line (24 Hours) 206-461-3222 Confidential crisis support available any time, day or night. www.crisisclinic.org
  • Alcohol/Drug 24-Hour Help Line 206-722-3700 • 1-800-562-1240 www.adhl.org
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 Free, confidential support for people in distress. Available 24/7.

HIV Testing & Sexual Health

  • Gay City Health Project 206-860-6969 HIV and STI testing, prevention services, and community health programs for LGBTQ+ people. www.gaycity.org
  • HIV/STD Hotline 206-205-7837 King County Public Health HIV/STD testing and information.

STI Clinics & General Health

  • Sexual Health Clinic at Harborview Comprehensive, LGBTQIA+ affirming public health clinic. 908 Jefferson St, Seattle.
  • The Spot (by Aradia) Free or low-cost HIV/STI testing and treatment for youth and young adults.
  • Cedar River Clinics Testing and treatment for Chlamydia, Gonorrhea, Herpes, HIV, and Syphilis. Seattle and surrounding areas.
  • MOD Clinic (Harborview) Low-barrier, walk-in HIV care and PrEP.
  • Public Health Centers Multiple locations including teen-specific clinics. Free or low-cost services.
  • UW Medicine Comprehensive health care with LGBTQ+ affirming services throughout the Seattle area.

Support & Funding

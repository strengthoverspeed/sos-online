Counseling & Treatment

  • Seattle Counseling Service 206-323-1768 Adult and youth treatment focusing on the LGBTQ+ community. www.seattlecounseling.org
  • Project NEON 206-323-1768 Free one-on-one counseling, support groups, and health info for crystal users. www.crystalneon.org
  • Entre Hermanos 206-322-7700 Support and resources for the Latino LGBTQ+ community. www.entrehermanos.org
  • POCAAN 206-322-7061 Community-based agency serving people of color. www.pocaan.org

Crisis & Help Lines

  • Crisis Line (24 Hours) 206-461-3222 Confidential crisis support available any time, day or night. www.crisisclinic.org
  • Alcohol/Drug 24-Hour Help Line 206-722-3700 • 1-800-562-1240 www.adhl.org
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline Call or text 988 Free, confidential support for people in distress. Available 24/7.

Support & Funding

SOS is made possible through the generosity of our community partners and supporters. We are grateful to those who help keep this resource free and accessible.

Want to support SOS? We’re an all-volunteer organization. If you or your organization would like to contribute to keeping SOS available to the community, please contact us at sosonlinemeetings@gmail.com.

About SOS

A grassroots community built by and for us

Strength Over Speed (SOS) began in 2005 as a group of peers who graduated from other crystal meth recovery programs but felt they needed something more. At the time, there were few options for queer men who wanted alternatives to 12-step programs.

Today, SOS is an all-volunteer, community-based project with multiple weekly peer-led support groups in Seattle, both in-person and online. We believe peer support is a foundational part of life in recovery.

We do not promote one path of recovery over another. We welcome everyone at any stage of their journey.
